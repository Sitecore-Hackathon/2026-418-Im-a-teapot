## 0.1 Feature Scope (Current vs. Coming)

### Current (in this release)
- **Manage revisions even within the same version** of a content piece (inspect, organize, and act within the item context).  
- **Track deletions in addition to creations and modifications** (deletion events included in the ledger).  
- **Sort and filter records of revisions** and **prepare exports** (note: **file download is blocked** when the widget runs in a **sandboxed iframe**, so the export control is currently disabled in that environment).  
- **Inspect revisions in the context of an item** (contextual panel within the item view).  
- **Dashboard widgets with statistics**: number of items published; fastest, slowest and average time from version creation to version published.  
- **Append‑only strategy** for the revision ledger (no destructive edits to the record).

### Coming (teaser)
- **Side‑by‑side compare of revisions** with color‑coded differences.  
- **Visual indications when linked items are deleted** (internal links, data sources, images, documents).  
- **Deletion & retention thresholds** (policy‑driven rules).  
- **Revert revisions** (one‑click restore to a selected revision).
